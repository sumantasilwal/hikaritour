Section 1

Hikari Tours is owned and managed by Mr Loknath Silwal, a qualified tourist and trekking guide with fifteen years plus experience. Over the years Mr Silwal has travelled through length and breadth of Nepal and has served hundreds of satisfied guests. Our staff members are professionally qualified and experienced to provide excellent service with smile to make you stay in Nepal truly memorable.Whatever your travel needs are we are here to help you

  • All inclusive package holidays to Nepal
  • International & domestic flights & hotels
  • Vehicle hire including Airport pickup & transfers
  • Mountain flights
  • City tours & sightseeing packages
  • Trekking, excursions and adventurous travel
  • White water rafting
  • Qualified tour & trekking guide hire

If you planning to visit Nepal in the near future please email your travel dates with a brief plan. We will revert to you with detailed itinerary and prices. We look forward to welcoming you in Nepal
